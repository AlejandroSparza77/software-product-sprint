My Background

Hello! My name is Sergio Alejandro Esparza González. I am from Mexico and I am currently studying a B.S. in Computer Science and Technology at ITESM.

I was born in Aguascalientes, Aguascalientes, Mexico on 09/21 twenty years ago.

I learned to use a computer since I was a child. My dad had a Windows 98 PC that I was able to learn on (a bit old even for those days).

Since I was a child I was interested in mathematics, I had more advanced knowledge than my schoolmates in it, of course I also had computers, because unfortunately the teachers did not even let us use them to do homework, until I entered high school (and only little by little).

I've played video games since I was little, I went from having an NES (also my dad's) to having a Wii (now mine), and then I had an Xbox One, but I've always played on PC anyway.

In middle school, my grades started to drop, because I was only focused on learning what I liked, so I started to focus almost entirely on school until I finished high school. This brought me many advantages, such as being the highest average of my generation, but it brought me disadvantages, such as not learning those things that I was passionate about (for example, I started programming shortly before entering university). Currently, I have tried to find a balance between the things I love and school, as proof of that I am here.

I also really like music, so much so that I play piano, guitar, recorder and I also sing; Similarly, I consider sports an important part of my life, since throughout my life I have practiced soccer, tae-kwon-do, swimming and basketball, in addition to training calisthenics.

My Hidden Talent

One talent that I have, however, I haven't developed as much, is acting. I have participated in some school plays, the last one I participated in was in late 2019 in high school (before the pandemic), called “PregonG19”, in which various pop culture characters appeared together in a fight of the good-bad , to find the "Secret of Perfection".

The protagonist was Saint Teresa of Ávila, in this work different stages of her life were covered, in which Dr. Strange, Mary Poppins, The Powerpuff Girls, Shrek, Indiana Jones, etc. appeared. And in my case, I was the Phantom of the Opera.

In this case, I was the only character on stage in the immediate stage after the protagonist passed away, giving a monologue about how the most important thing that defines a person is not what they have, but what they do. and how was the legacy that she left due to her actions in life.

I have also acted in Hamlet's "To be, or not to be" monologue, I acted as a psychologist in a project for Semana Tec at the university, as Agustín de Iturbide in a play about the Independence of Mexico, and I have also been a narrator of various school videos and host of events (although this is not acting as such, many of the fundamentals apply).
